About Us

GreenHarvest Agrochem (I) Pvt Ltd

Our Infrastructure

GreenHarvest Agrochem (I) Pvt. Ltd. is located in Maharashtra State, in the village Takawade near the city of Ichalkaranji in Kolhapur District. It is 400 km south of Nhava Sheva Port, Mumbai and is well connected by road, rail & air.

Mr. Netaji Powar, MD of the Company, is a post graduate chemical engineer from UDCT (now ICT), Mumbai and has a vast industrial experience. A well groomed, technically sound and dedicated team is the backbone of any industry. The people at GreenHarvest Agrochem (I) Pvt. Ltd. are from various streams of the industry with wide experience in their respective fields. Various departments viz. projects, production, quality assurance, quality control, research and development, marketing and finance are headed by professional chemical engineers, science graduates, post graduates and chartered accountants.

The production facility at Takawade is designed to produce approximately 100 MT/ day of fertilizers. The facility consists of SS & PP-FRP reactors, centrifuges, storage tanks, pulverizes, grinders, ribbon blenders, fluid bed dryers, spray dryer, liquid filling machines, powder packing machines etc. Their capacities and numbers are adequate to produce the required quantity.

Indian Farmers Crop Lab

Laboratory - Soil, Water & Plant Testing

GreenHarvest Agro's Indian Farmers Crop Lab is working for Indian Farmers mainly with the 'Grape farmers'. Most of the Indian farmers use or apply fertilizers to their fields without testing soil and without considering plant's requirements. Very few Indian farmers do agriculture on a commercial basis with proper technical guidance. GreenHarvest Agro's main goal while developing the Indian Farmers Crop Lab was to create awareness in Indian Farmers for using fertilizers only when the plants require them depending on their growth stages and age.

Improper use of fertilizers leads to inconsistent quality of farm produce and heavy financial burden on the farmers. This is a particularly evident in the case of Wine Industry which suffers a lot due to inconsistent quality of grapes. This is where GreenHarvest Agro's Crop Lab works to test soil, water and plant to give proper nutrients to the crop resulting in improved yield and consistent quality. This will further improve farmers earnings which will become a motivating factor for the young generation to take up agricultural work.

Considering the overall agricultural conditions and farmers requirements, GreenHarvest Agro has developed this 'State-of-the-art' Crop Lab with excellent infrastructure and most advanced testing equipment along with dedicated and trained staff to extend support to the farmers which will ultimately transform the entire agriculture sector contributing to the national growth.
